On this website you can manage your big image collection by analysing yor images through deep learning methods. In the following will be described what you can do on this website.

How to manage your image collection:

  • Under Upload you can upload your images.
  • Under Images you can manage your uploaded Images manually.
  • Under Aesthetics you can analyze your images for aesthetics. Every Image gets an corresponging aesthetic score, so you can find your most aesthetic images.
  • Under Faces you can analyze your images for upcoming faces. Your Images with found faces will be shown and sorted by the number of faces found in the images.
  • Under Tags you can analyze your images for 600 categories. Your images will be tagged by the found categories. After the analyzis you can see a list of all the found categories and will be able to click on them to see the corresponding images.
  • Under NDD (Near Duplicate Detection) you can analyze your images for duplicates, so you can delete the unwanted duplicates in your image collection.
  • Under Convenience you can analyze your images to find all the convenient images in your collection, like old bills, screenshots, messages, ... and delete those that you no longer need.
